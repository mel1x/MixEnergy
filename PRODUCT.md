# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Minecraft players configuring their local HUD and single-player gameplay, plus
server administrators balancing energy rules through the common config.

## Product Purpose

MixEnergy adds an understandable stamina system to Minecraft. Its settings
surface should make HUD placement and gameplay balance quick to inspect and
safe to change without hiding which values are controlled by a server.

## Brand Personality

Game-native, restrained, dependable.

## Anti-references

Avoid web-dashboard styling, decorative glass effects, oversized controls,
ambiguous icons, and layouts that collide at Minecraft's smallest supported GUI
scales.

## Design Principles

- Keep controls visually consistent with Minecraft's established interaction patterns.
- Show the effect of spatial settings directly where the choice is made.
- Separate local presentation from server-authoritative gameplay values.
- Preserve dense layouts without sacrificing readable labels or click targets.
- Save single-player changes immediately and communicate disabled states clearly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Do not rely on color alone for selection or enabled state. Keep text and icons
legible at small GUI scales, preserve keyboard navigation, and avoid decorative
motion.
